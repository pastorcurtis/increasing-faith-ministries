Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

Increasing Faith Ministries ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at increasingfaith.net or interact with our ministry.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, submit a prayer request, or contact us.
  • Prayer Requests: The content of prayer requests you submit, along with any optional contact information you provide.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Basic analytics data such as pages visited, time spent on site, and general location (city/country level only).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and prayer requests
  • Send you newsletter updates and Kingdom teachings (if subscribed)
  • Improve our website and ministry services
  • Share prayer requests with our prayer team (only if you opt-in)

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Prayer Request Confidentiality

We treat all prayer requests with the utmost confidentiality and care:

  • Prayer requests are only shared with our pastoral team and prayer partners
  • If you choose to share your request with others, it will be shared anonymously unless you specify otherwise
  • We do not publish or publicly share prayer requests without explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption on all pages
  • Secure email handling
  • Limited access to personal data

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Netlify: Website hosting and deployment
  • Google Fonts: Typography (no personal data collected)
  • YouTube: Embedded video content (subject to YouTube's privacy policy)
  • Givelify: Online giving platform (subject to Givelify's privacy policy)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
