Kingdom Community

Not an organization you join—a people under a King. Find your place in the global movement.

A Global Movement

One Kingdom. Every Nation.

Across cultures, ethnicities, and borders, God is forming a family whose shared allegiance is to Jesus, not a denomination, ideology, or nation-state.

Local

Rooted Community

Kingdom community always has a local expression. It looks like people who:

  • Share life, not just services
  • Practice hospitality, generosity, confession, and prayer
  • Carry one another's burdens and celebrate growth
The kingdom takes root in ordinary places through faithful presence.

Global

Kingdom Vision

Local communities are never isolated. They see themselves as part of a global body advancing the same reign of God.

  • Valuing every culture as a bearer of God's image
  • Learning from believers across the world
  • Aligning with God's purposes beyond comfort
The kingdom expands not by control, but by shared obedience to the same King.

Eternal

Impact

Kingdom community measures success differently. Not attendance. Not buildings. Not budgets.

  • Lives restored
  • Justice practiced
  • Reconciliation embodied
  • Faithfulness sustained over time
What is built in love, obedience, and truth outlasts this age.
Small Group Formation

Discipleship Cohorts

To form disciples who live under the reign of Jesus—together.

This is not information transfer. It is life apprenticeship.
Cohort Size
5-8 people
Rhythm
Weekly (90 min)
Posture
Honest, Scripture-centered

Cohort Commitments

Each participant commits to:

Presence

Show up consistently

Practice

Live out what is learned

Participation

Speak truth and listen well

Prayer

Intercede for one another

Submission

Allow the Spirit and community to shape you

Weekly Gathering Flow

90 minutes of intentional formation

10 min

Recenter

Silence, Scripture reading, short prayer

"Jesus, we submit again to Your reign."

20 min

Shared Life

Highs, lows, and faith-in-action from the week

Where did you notice the Kingdom? Where did you resist it?

30 min

Scripture & Truth

Engage Jesus' teaching together

What is Jesus revealing about life under God's rule?

20 min

Practice & Obedience

Discuss concrete responses

Decide on one kingdom practice for the week

10 min

Prayer & Commissioning

Pray with expectation

Send one another out as representatives of the Kingdom
