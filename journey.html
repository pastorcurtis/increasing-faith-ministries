For Believers

Grow as a Kingdom Citizen

You've entered the Kingdom—now it's time to be formed as a disciple who lives under Jesus' lordship.

01

Root Yourself in Scripture

The Word of God shapes Kingdom citizens. Develop rhythms of reading, studying, and meditating on Scripture daily.

02

Join a Discipleship Cohort

Transformation happens in community. Join a small group for accountability, encouragement, and deeper formation.

03

Practice Kingdom Disciplines

Prayer, fasting, generosity, service—these practices align our lives with Kingdom values and priorities.

04

Discover Your Kingdom Assignment

Every believer has a sphere of influence. Learn to carry Kingdom authority into your work, family, and community.

05

Make Disciples Who Make Disciples

The ultimate goal: reproduce Kingdom life in others. Learn to invest in and develop emerging disciples.

Kingdom Foundations

A comprehensive teaching series through the essential teachings of Jesus about life in His Kingdom.

01The King and the Kingdom

Text: Mark 1:14-15

The gospel Jesus preached was the Kingdom of God.

02Entering the Kingdom

Text: John 3:1-8

You must be born again to see and enter the Kingdom.

03Kingdom Citizens

Text: Matthew 5:1-16

The Beatitudes describe Kingdom character.

04Kingdom Righteousness

Text: Matthew 5:17-48

Kingdom ethics exceed external compliance.

05Kingdom Practices

Text: Matthew 6:1-18

Giving, praying, and fasting in secret.

06Kingdom Priorities

Text: Matthew 6:19-34

Seek first the Kingdom and His righteousness.

07Kingdom Relationships

Text: Matthew 7:1-12

How Kingdom citizens treat one another.

08Kingdom Choices

Text: Matthew 7:13-29
